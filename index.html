About Me

I'm a bioinformatics researcher with a passion for leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning to solve complex biological problems. With a Master's in Bioinformatics and a background in Computer Science, I bring a unique blend of computational expertise and biological understanding to my research. As a co-founder of Gene-ius Coders and an active participant in academic pursuits, I'm committed to advancing the field of bioinformatics and fostering collaboration within the scientific community.
